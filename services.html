1. Support request

2. We do our magic

We know time is valuable. We don’t want to waste yours. So we’ll make sure your request is handled quickly and efficiently. We will keep you updated on our progress.

We will test to make sure your website will function properly after we’re done with it. You will able to check immediately and give us your feedback. In case the changes aren’t as expected, we can go back to the original so there are no risks for you.
