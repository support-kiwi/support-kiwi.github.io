Nice to meet you

support.kiwi is a client-service focused website maintenance company supporting sites based on the WordPress and Magento content management systems, as well as custom ones. We like to code and we have an eye for the detail. If we see a visual bug, we’ll squash it too!

support.kiwi is all about helping small and mid-sized businesses keep their websites safe, fast, functional and up-to-date. We understand what’s important and we prioritize our work. We understand every website needs are different, so we personalize our offer for you.

We are a small team of developers with years of experience and a true passion for website maintenance and support. Our job is to keep your website online, secure, fast, bug-free and up-to-date. And we really do love doing this!

You can count on us to give you our best advice regarding your website’s security and performance. We promise to always be honest and straightforward.
